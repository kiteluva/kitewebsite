PROJECT 1: Predictive Modeling of Spotify Songs (Python)

Developed and compared advanced predictive models (XGBoost, RandomForest, and Gradient Boosting Machines) to forecast the popularity of Spotify's top songs. PROJECT 2: Song Popularity Regression & Classification (R)

Implemented both regression and classification models in R to predict Spotify song popularity and its probability class. For regression, I evaluated Random Forest, GBM, and XGBoost models using R-squared and RMSE, demonstrating their effectiveness in estimating popularity scores. For classification, AUC and ROC curves were utilized to assess the performance of XGBoost and Random Forest models in categorizing songs into popularity classes, providing a comprehensive view of factors influencing virality.
